Our Story

Why We
Started

Moving to Germany is exciting. Navigating its financial system alone is not.

The pension system, insurance landscape, and investment regulations are complex even for native Germans. For someone managing obligations across two countries, two currencies, and two tax systems, the complexity multiplies.

We started Bharat Finance because we saw how many expats were making important financial decisions without proper guidance. Our goal is simple: help you understand your options, choose the right products, and build a financial plan that works whether you stay in Germany or move on.
